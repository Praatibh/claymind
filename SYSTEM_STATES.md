## ğŸ¨ Visual Patterns for System States

### Color Coding

**Success states:**
- Background: Mint Green tint (#4ECDC4 at 10% opacity)
- Icon: Green checkmark
- Border: Soft green

**Error states:**
- Background: Coral Orange tint (#FF6B6B at 10% opacity)
- Icon: Orange warning triangle or info circle
- Border: Soft orange
- NO red (too alarming for kids)

**Info/Loading states:**
- Background: Electric Blue tint (#2D9CDB at 10% opacity)
- Icon: Blue spinner or info
- Border: Soft blue

**Warning/Confirmation states:**
- Background: Sunrise Yellow tint (#F9D56E at 10% opacity)
- Icon: Yellow caution symbol
- Border: Soft yellow

---

### Animation Patterns

**Loading:**
- Smooth spinner (1.5s rotation)
- Optional Clai idle animation
- Subtle pulse on text

**Error appears:**
- Gentle shake (2-3 times)
- Fade in from top
- Icon bounces once

**Success appears:**
- Scale up from center
- Confetti/sparkles (for major wins)
- Fade in with bounce

**Toast notifications:**
- Slide in from top-right
- Stay 3-4 seconds
- Slide out or fade
- Can be dismissed early

---

This comprehensive system states guide ensures every edge case, error, loading state, and empty state has clear, empathetic, age-appropriate microcopy that helps users understand what's happening and what to do next.
